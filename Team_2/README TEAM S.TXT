# 🪙 GoldVault – Live Gold Price Tracker

**GoldVault** is a modern, real-time web application designed for investors and gold enthusiasts to track live XAU (Gold) market data. Whether you're monitoring global spot prices, calculating the value of specific jewelry karats, or tracking your personal portfolio, GoldVault provides a premium, data-driven experience.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📊 Real-Time Market Data
- **Live XAU Ticker**: A continuous scrolling bar showing the latest gold prices in USD and JOD.
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Seamlessly toggle between USD ($) and Jordanian Dinar (JD) across the entire application.
- **Dynamic Price Cards**: View instant rates for **24K, 21K, and 18K** gold.

### 📈 Historical Analysis
- **Interactive Price Chart**: Powered by **Chart.js**, offering a 30-day view of gold price fluctuations.
- **Custom Date Filters**: Analyze market trends by selecting specific date ranges.

### 💍 Specialized Asset Tracking
- **Jewelry & Coins**: Specific pricing for **Rashadi** and **English** coins.
- **Gold Bars**: Comprehensive price tables for various bar sizes (1g up to 1kg).
- **Gold Calculator**: Instantly calculate the value of physical gold based on weight and karat.

### 💼 Portfolio Management ("My Assets")
- **Personal Vault**: Users can securely log in to track their physical gold holdings.
- **Profit/Loss Tracking**: Real-time calculation of portfolio value based on current market rates.

### 📰 Market Insights
- **News Carousel**: Integrated with the **GNews API** to provide the latest headlines affecting the gold market.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: 
  - Semantic **HTML5**
  - **CSS3** (Modern Vanilla for branding + **Bootstrap 5** for layout and components)
  - **JavaScript (ES6+)** for logic and API orchestration.
- **Data Visualization**: [Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/)
- **Authentication**: Custom JS-based auth system with persistent storage.
- **APIs**:
  - **Gold-API.com**: Live and historical gold price data.
  - **GNews API**: Real-time global financial news.
